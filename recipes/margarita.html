The Margartia

Description

The margarita is a classic cocktail made with tequila, triple sec and lime. A great drink for the summer!

Ingredients (one glass)

  • ice
  • 50ml tequila
  • 25ml lime juice
  • 20ml triple sec
  • salt

Steps

  1. Sprinkle salt over a plate and rub the rim of glass with lime wedge, twist the rim on the salt getting an even coating around the top edge of the glass
  2. Add all ingredients and ice to a shaker and shake till it feels cold
  3. pour through strainer into a chilled glass containing a large ice cube
  4. Drink!
