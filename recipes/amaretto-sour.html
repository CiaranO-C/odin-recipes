The Amaretto Sour

Description

The Amaretto sour is Like an elegantly spiked lemonade. However, its distinctive nuttiness makes the cocktail all the more complex, it can be a real crowd splitter.

Ingredients (one glass)

  • 1/2 shot of egg white
  • A dash of bitters
  • A single shot of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Two shots of Disaronno amaretto

Steps

  1. Shake with all Ingredients in shaker with ice (20 seconds)
  2. Strain into another shaker without ice and shake (10 seconds)
  3. pour through strainer into a chilled glass containing a large ice cube
  4. Drink!
