The Basil Smash

Description

The Basil Smash is a vibrant, zingy, refreshing cocktail which not only tastes great but is ludicrously simple to make. Prepare to smash!

Ingredients (one glass)

  • 5 fresh basil leaves (although more never hurts!)
  • A single shot of sugar syrup
  • A single shot of freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • Two shots of gin

Steps

  1. Place basil leaves inside of cocktail shaker and muddle until leaves just start to go dull. (Don't over smash here!)
  2. Pop a few ice cubes into the shaker
  3. Pour the syrup, lemon juice and gin into the shaker.. the order doesn't matter!
  4. Shake vigorously for around 15-20 seconds
  5. pour through strainer into a chilled glass containing a large ice cube
  6. Drink!
